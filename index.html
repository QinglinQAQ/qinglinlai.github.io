    About Me

    Hongyi Luo is working toward the M.Sc. degree in the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China, under the supervision of Dr. Guyue Li . His current research interests include physical layer security, radio frequency fingerprint identification, secret key generation, and reconfigurable intelligent surface.
